<p><strong>The Machine Vision Area Scan Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Line Scan Camera</li><li>Area Scan Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The machine vision camera market is primarily categorized into line scan cameras and area scan cameras. Line scan cameras capture images one line at a time, making them ideal for applications requiring high-speed imaging of continuous objects, such as in web or textile inspection. In contrast, area scan cameras capture complete 2D images in a single shot, suitable for stationary objects like parts inspection and barcode reading. Both camera types play crucial roles in industrial automation and quality control processes.</p></p>
