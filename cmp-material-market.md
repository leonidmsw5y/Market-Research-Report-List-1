<p><ul><li>CMP Pads</li><li>CMP Slurries</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CMP (Chemical Mechanical Planarization) material market consists of two primary types: CMP pads and CMP slurries. CMP pads are abrasives utilized to polish semiconductor wafers, facilitating uniform surface finishing. These pads are designed for durability and efficiency, impacting the overall polishing process. CMP slurries, on the other hand, are chemically formulated mixtures containing abrasives and chemicals that aid in the planarization of various substrates. Together, they play a crucial role in semiconductor fabrication and various other applications in microelectronics.</p></p>
