<p><p>The PVC pipe market comprises various types, including Chlorinated PVC (CPVC) pipes, Unplasticized PVC (uPVC) pipes, and Plasticized PVC (pPVC) pipes. CPVC pipes are known for their higher temperature resistance, making them ideal for hot water applications. uPVC pipes are rigid and commonly used for drainage and plumbing due to their durability and resistance to corrosion. pPVC pipes, which contain plasticizers, offer flexibility and are often used in applications requiring bendability. Each type serves distinct purposes in construction and plumbing sectors.</p></p>
