<p><p>The MEMS air velocity sensor market includes various types such as mechanical and ultrasonic air velocity sensors. In contrast, ultrasonic air velocity sensors employ sound waves to determine air velocity, providing non-invasive and highly accurate measurements. Both types serve critical applications in HVAC systems, environmental monitoring, and industrial processes, driving demand for precise airflow measurement technologies.</p></p>
