<p><ul><li>PSA Nitrogen Generators</li><li>Membrane Nitrogen Generators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nitrogen generation market comprises two main types: PSA (Pressure Swing Adsorption) nitrogen generators and membrane nitrogen generators. PSA generators use adsorbent materials to separate nitrogen from air by cycling between high and low pressures, producing high-purity nitrogen suitable for various industrial applications. In contrast, membrane nitrogen generators utilize selective permeability of membranes to separate nitrogen from other gases, offering a compact and efficient solution suitable for applications with lower purity requirements. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
