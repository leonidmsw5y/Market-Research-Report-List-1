<p><p>Signage lighting can be categorized into three main types: front glowing, side glowing, and back glowing. Front glowing fixtures illuminate the surface of the sign, enhancing visibility and colors directly. Side glowing lighting casts a glow from the edges, creating a subtle and stylish effect that highlights the sign's shape. Back glowing, or halo lighting, emits light from behind the sign, generating a soft glow that emphasizes the sign's outline and adds depth, making it visually appealing, especially at night.</p></p>
