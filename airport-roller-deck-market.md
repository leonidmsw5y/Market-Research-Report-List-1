Belt Connected roller decks utilize flexible belts to transport luggage and cargo, offering smooth and quiet operation. In contrast, Chain Connected systems use chains and rollers for enhanced durability and strength, ideal for handling heavier loads. Both types are essential for efficient baggage handling, ensuring quick movement throughout terminals. Their selection depends on specific operational needs, such as load capacity, maintenance requirements, and overall efficiency.</p></p>
