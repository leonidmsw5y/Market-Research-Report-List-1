<p><ul><li>Brick Refractories</li><li>Monolithic Refractories</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steel refractory material market is primarily categorized into two types: brick refractories and monolithic refractories. Brick refractories are pre-formed, rigid products made from various refractory materials, utilized for lining furnaces and kilns due to their durability and thermal stability. Conversely, monolithic refractories are plastic or castable materials that can be easily shaped and applied, providing seamless and flexible installation. Both types serve essential roles in steel production, protecting equipment from extreme temperatures and harsh environments.</p></p>
