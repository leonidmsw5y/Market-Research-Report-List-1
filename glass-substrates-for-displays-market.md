<p><p>The glass substrates market for displays includes three primary types: LCD, LED, and OLED displays. LED (Light Emitting Diode) substrates are used in backlighting for LCDs and standalone displays for enhanced brightness and energy efficiency. OLED (Organic Light Emitting Diode) substrates enable flexible and thinner screens with vibrant colors and improved contrast, making them popular for high-end devices and televisions. Each type supports distinct technological advancements.</p></p>
