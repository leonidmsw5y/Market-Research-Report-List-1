<p><ul><li>High Methoxyl (HM) Pectin</li><li>Low Methoxyl (LM) Pectin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Low Sugar Pectin Amidated market is categorized primarily into High Methoxyl (HM) Pectin and Low Methoxyl (LM) Pectin. HM Pectin requires higher sugar concentrations to gel and is used in products with higher sugar content, providing a robust texture. Conversely, LM Pectin forms gels with lower sugar levels and is ideal for low-sugar or sugar-free applications, allowing for versatility in food products. Both types serve distinct roles in food processing, particularly in jams, jellies, and other confectionery.</p></p>
